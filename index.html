About

My Bloody Valentine are an Irish-English alternative rock band formed in Dublin in 1983 and consisting since 1987. Often cited as a pioneering act in the shoegaze genre, their sound is characterized by dissonant guitar textures, subdued and androgynous vocals, and unorthodox production techniques.
